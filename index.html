About Us

Lavay's Good Food Restaurant is a food and love catering club which caters for the community and also the internationals that come to enjoy Lavay's good food from time to time. The restaurant is located in the heart of Khayelitsha, in the Mandela Park district. It was established in 2019 September where the restaurant opened with a creation of new school urban burgers that could only be found in the surbabian are and not in the townships. It then that people in the townships had to no longer travel in order for them get served good food at a standard price. Lavay's Good Food Restaurant was then able to build up more dishes on the menu to ensure that it keeps up with its standard of service good food cuisine. In 2020, 03 October it became a force to be reckoned with its exclusive Uefa Champions League Fifa Games Tournament which has built a great following of up to 200 customers on a weekly basis. The popularity of this venue has grown and demands the attention as an entertainment hot spot where it has held numerous events to ensure that people from around the gets to know about this newly discovered restaurant in the township. The restaurant has also been able to create job opportunities for those less fortunate to find jobs because of their lack of experience in the career fields. The business, due to its success on Facebook apps and services, currently has four full-time staff, which increases to about thirty people when they have an event.
